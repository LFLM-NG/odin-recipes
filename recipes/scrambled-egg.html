Scrambled Eggs

Description

Simple scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, crack in eggs
  2. Add in salt and beat the eggs to desired consistency
  3. In a pan, heat cooking oil on medium-high heat until the oil starts to shimmer
  4. Add in beaten eggs
  5. Cook and stir and eggs are set, between 3 to 5 minutes
