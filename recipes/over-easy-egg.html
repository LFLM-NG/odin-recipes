Over Easy Eggs

Description

Simple over easy eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat cooking oil on medium-high heat until the oil starts to shimmer
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan
  3. Once the whites become slightly firm and the yolk starts to set, or about 2 minutes, flip the eggs
  4. Cook for another 30 to 60 seconds; season with salt and pepper to taste
