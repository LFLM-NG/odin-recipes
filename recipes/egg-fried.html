Fried Egg Recipe

Description

Simple Sunny Sides

Add Lots of Oil if It Gets Too Healthy

Ingredients

  • Eggs (As many as pan can reasonably fit)
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in medium heat pan; fry eggs until the egg white show color
  2. Doneness varies, typically between 1 to 3 minutes; cover with a lid for better control
