Boiled Eggs Recipe

Description

If You Can Boil Water, You Can Boil Eggs

Add Lots of Oil if It Gets Too Healthy

Ingredients

  • Eggs (As many as pot can reasonably fit)
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Bring water to a rolling boil in a pot; turn the heat down to simmering
  2. Put the eggs in so that the water can cover the eggs entirely
  3. Doneness varies, typically between 6 to 13 minutes; shock in ice bath to stop cooking if you want runnier eggs
