Fried Tofu Recipe

Description

If You Can Boil Water, You Can Boil Eggs

Add Lots of Salt if It Gets Too Healthy

Ingredients

  • 1 Block of Tofu (Firm Preferred)

Steps

  1. Press liquid out of tofu; freeze and thaw tofu beforehand for better results
  2. Cut tofu into desired size
  3. Heat oil in medium heat pan; fry tofu until golden brown (time varies depending on size, typically between 1 to 3 minutes)
